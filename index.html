How it Works

Our algorithm is built on three scientific pillars to ensure total body recovery.

Baseline

We start with 33ml per kg. This is the vital amount needed for your organs to function at rest.

Gender Lift

Males receive a 10.5% increase to support higher muscle-to-water ratios.

Sweat Fix

We add 350ml for every 30m of movement to replace what you lose on the activity or in the gym.
