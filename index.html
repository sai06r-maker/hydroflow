Purpose & Vision

The Mission

Our mission is to promote healthy living by helping individuals understand and meet their daily hydration needs through a simple, accurate, and accessible digital hydration calculator. We aim to empower people of all ages to build better water-drinking habits using science-based recommendations and personalized insights.

The Vision

Our vision is to become a globally trusted hydration companion that encourages healthier lifestyles, prevents dehydration-related health issues, and uses smart technology to make wellness simple, engaging, and achievable for everyone.

Scientific Logic

The Baseline Formula

The baseline formula calculates daily water needs using body weight. It multiplies weight (kg) by about 35 ml to estimate how much water the body needs under normal conditions.

Dynamic Adjustments

Gender can influence hydration because males generally have higher muscle mass and lower body fat compared to females. Since muscle contains more water than fat, males often require slightly more daily water on average,also male profiles receive 10.5% scaling lift.

The Impact

athletes

A hydration calculator helps athletes maintain peak performance by providing personalized fluid intake guidance based on activity level and conditions. Proper hydration improves endurance, recovery, and reduces the risk of fatigue and heat-related illness.

individuals

For regular individuals, a hydration calculator supports overall health by encouraging sufficient daily water intake. It helps improve energy, focus, and digestion while preventing headaches and fatigue.
